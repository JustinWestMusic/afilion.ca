Presets FAQ – Help

NI Massive

Why can’t I open the preset?

You either have a version older than 1.1.5 that cannot open the presets or you have a newer version in which case you must batch convert it or choose import ksd.

My Massive only opens .nmsv files

In this case you must batch convert your preset(s) or import them individually in the drop down menu by clicking on import KSD sound.

Sylenth1

Why can’t I open the preset?

You have a version older than 2.2 that cannot open the preset. You must choose to load preset .fxp.

What is the difference between .fxb and .fxp?
  • .fxb are bank files and contain multiple presets
  • .fxp is a single preset file
