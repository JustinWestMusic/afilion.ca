Released: August 2016

NEW DAYS symbolizes, new brighter days everyone has after periods of dark ones. When a few days didnâ€™t go so well you know there will be several new ones that will go your way. With that being said NEW DAYS encompasses themes of exploration, self-improvement, relaxation and fun! The album's tracks blend many of the following genres: Chiptune, Trance, Progressive House, Electro House, Tropical House, Future Bass, Jazz, Funk, Synth-pop, Electronica and Dance. NEW DAYS has a vast array of influences but to narrow it down it is heavily influenced by retro video game, 80's and modern progressive dance music.
