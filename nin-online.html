Nin Online Soundtrack

Released: May 2016

Over the past few years I have been composing and and producing the soundtrack for he Naruto-inspired MMORPG Nin Online. For this project I did not use any pre-existing Naruto music as reference but rather I experimented with synthesizers and real Japanese instruments in different styles that I felt best suited the various environments. I also made sure that each composition had at least over one minute of melodic and rhytmic variation as to not bore or tire the players ears.
