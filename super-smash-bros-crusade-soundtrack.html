Super Smash Bros. Crusade Soundtrack

Released: April 2015

Super Smash Bros. Crusade was my very first game project and probably the most demanding one too. Between 2013 and 2015 I composed over two hours worth of demo material, some of which is included in the soundtrack for free download. Even though Super Smash Bros. Crusade is a fan game I treated seriously like all my other projects. Other than specifying this soundtrack as simply "game music", I was actually after a specific sound which I found was absent in the original Super Smash Bros. series.

Although I am not saying I hit the nail on the head, I think I came pretty close to developing my EDM/Orchestral Hybrid music I had in mind for this game. Where appropriate, most of the electronic songs are very upbeat and fun, while the orchestral songs are more uplifting and melodic. As previously mentioned this soundtrack contains some of my demos. Unfortunately or fortunately, however you want to perceive it, this soundtrack contains only songs I have composed/ReMixed or collaborated on.
